About Fur Baby Grooming Studio

A Monroe, MI grooming studio built on a simple belief: every pet deserves to be treated with patience, skill, and love.

Why We Do What We Do

Fur Baby Grooming Studio was born out of a lifelong passion for animals and a desire to provide Monroe-area pet owners with a grooming experience they can genuinely trust. We know how stressful grooming can be — for both owners and pets — so we've built our entire approach around reducing anxiety and making every visit as positive as possible.

Whether your dog is a first-time grooming client or a seasoned pro, we treat them with the same gentle care and professional attention. We take pride in breed-specific styling, thorough sanitation, and honest communication with every single client.
